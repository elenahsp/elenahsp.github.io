My Work

Magazine Layout

Simple, made to be easy on your eyes, with the most important things written in red, the only present color in the magazine. The red color is carefully placed for the purpose of making the important things easily noticeable without being too agressive.

Catalogue Design

A catalogue about fictional honey store called Vita Bee, full of pastel shades that are associated with honey, peace and harmony. The catalogue is meant to tell a cute story about the products.

Personal Business Cards

Minimalistic cartoon version of myself drawn in my regular drawing style because I like keeping things simple, but not boring.

Logo Design

Logo redesign for an art supplies store. The beauty of the artistic espression represented in the most minimalistic way.
